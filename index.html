My Interest?

My designs and builds are driven by my deep passion for making. To me, a build’s outcome doesn’t matter as long as it brings a smile to someone's face. I have created electronic toys for kids, teaching kits for my peers, and applications to make life easier for anyone who uses it. I have always felt that in order to be a successful programmer, I need to first understand the hardware it is programmed on. We can only understand why some protocols are made or created if we understand the hardware limitations. This is why I have chosen computer engineering as my field of study: to learn both hardware and software skills.

Aside from making, I love nature and believe in leading an active lifestyle through climbing, cycling, and badminton. If you want to chat about programming, robotics, projects, or anything else, don’t hesitate to connect with me through LinkedIn on the right!
